# The 3 Richest People on Earth Right Now

They didn't inherit their wealth. They built it from scratch. And the numbers are almost impossible to comprehend.

Number three is Larry Ellison, the founder of Oracle. His net worth sits at around 150 billion dollars. He built his empire selling database software to corporations, and he has never stopped grinding. Even in his eighties, he still shows up to work every single day.

Number two is Jeff Bezos, the man who turned a garage bookstore into the largest shopping platform on the planet. Amazon now generates over 500 billion dollars in annual revenue. Bezos stepped down as CEO, but his wealth keeps climbing. Currently sitting at around 200 billion dollars.

And number one, the undisputed richest human alive, is Elon Musk. His combined holdings in Tesla, SpaceX, and X put him somewhere between 300 and 400 billion dollars depending on the day. Love him or hate him, no one in history has ever accumulated this much wealth this fast.

Three men. Three completely different paths. One insane result. The gap between them and everyone else has never been wider.
